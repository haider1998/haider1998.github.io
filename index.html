About Me

My journey into technology wasn't sparked in a lecture hall, but by a fascination with how things work, taking apart electronics as a child just to understand their core. This innate drive to dissect, comprehend, and build led me to create my first website for my father's business at 13, revealing the power of technology to solve real-world problems and ignite positive change.

This passion has defined my career. As a Senior Software Engineer, I thrive on tackling complex challenges in demanding environments like the financial and automotive sectors. At HSBC, I architected enterprise microservices for the Kinetic platform generating over \$1 billion annually, optimizing performance by 5x and reducing infrastructure costs by 20%. I also developed an NLP-powered AI solution using OpenAI and LangChain that cut SQL query generation time by 80%, significantly improving data accessibility.

Currently at Ford, I'm spearheading the development of a large-scale data product managing 25 PB of data. My focus on automation led to a tool that improved feed setup efficiency by 200x, saving \$120,000 annually. These experiences highlight my commitment to delivering measurable business value through innovative, scalable, and efficient software solutions.

Beyond the code, I'm driven by a desire to continuously learn and contribute. Whether it's mentoring new joiners as an official Buddy at HSBC, tutoring computer science concepts, sharing knowledge as a Subject Expert at Chegg, exploring the potential of Generative AI, or contributing to open-source projects like PyVisualizer and PipelineIQ, I believe in the power of collaboration and pushing technological boundaries. My goal is to leverage technology to build impactful solutions that are not only technically excellent but also user-centric and beneficial.

My personal interests, like cooking, traveling, cycling, and playing badminton, often provide fresh perspectives on problem-solving, reminding me that creativity and structure can coexist beautifully. I'm excited by the prospect of applying my skills and experience to new challenges at forward-thinking organizations.
